Online job-search safety affects how people protect accounts, data, devices, or everyday work. Many problems do not begin with one major technical failure. They begin when a small decision is made too quickly or without enough information. Scammers can imitate a company, interview process, or onboarding portal to request fees, identity data, bank details, or help moving money.

## Why online job-search safety deserves attention
